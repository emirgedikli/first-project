When referring to her abilities, Viper refers to toxic chemicals as "toxins". Technically, this is a misnomer, as the term "Toxin" refers to harmful biological compounds that can be found in nature and are produced by living organisms. For artificially manufactured products with similar properties, the term "Toxicant" should be used. However, due to his advanced scientific training, Viper probably knows this, but nevertheless calls the poison a "toxin". This implies that the chemicals are, at least in part, derived from natural poisons (this is speculation, not canon). Viper's most distinguishing characteristic is its dark past. At one point in the past, Viper was a brilliant chemist, in a position of esteem due to her great mind. According to her file published by Fade, Viper has also received numerous awards (including the R. Francis Award and the Danton Award for outstanding innovation). According to an Omen voice line, she specializes in medicine and healing in one form or another. "Think about it, Sabine...you used to heal with your talent. It's funny." - Omen
